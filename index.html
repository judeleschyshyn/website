Bio.

Jude Leschyshyn (rhymes with "musician") is a pianist and saxophonist based in Saskatoon on Treaty 6 territory. He holds a B.Mus. and Jazz Certificate from the University of Saskatchewan. He works as a piano teacher, performer, and composer.

Lessons.

With fifteen years of piano experience, Jude teaches piano, music theory, and music history to all ages and skill levels. His private studio is located on Saskatoon's east side. He is currently accepting new students—enquire here.

Gigs.

Jude performs with the Saskatoon Experimental Music Ensemble and Footnotes Big Band. His work has been featured as part of the Strata Festival of New Music and the Saskatchewan Jazz Festival.
